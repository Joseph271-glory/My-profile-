EDUCATION

JOURNEY OF EDUCATION

Education is the process of learning and gaining knowledge, skills, and values. It is called a journey because it doesn't happen in one day.

Education is not only reading books in school. It also includes learning how to think, behave, solve problems, and live with other people.

1. Beginning of Education (Home):
The journey of education usually starts at home. Before a child goes to school, parents or family members teach simple things like:

  • How to speak
  • How to behave
  • How to greet people
  • What is right and wrong

2. Early Schooling:
After learning basic things at home, the child goes to school where real learning begins. At this stage, teachers help children learn:

  • How to read and write
  • How to count numbers
  • How to communicate with others

3. Secondary Education:
As the child grows older, education becomes deeper and more challenging. In secondary school, students are introduced to subjects like:

  • Further Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
